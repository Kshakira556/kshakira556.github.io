About Me

Hi, I’m Shakira Knight, a mobile and web developer with a passion for building technology that keeps people safe, connected, and empowered.

From Sales to Software

Before diving into tech, I spent years in business operations and enterprise sales, leading national teams, managing major accounts, and driving growth for brands like Vodacom, MTN, and iKhokha. That experience taught me how to spot opportunities, communicate across silos, and solve problems that matter.

Identity & Mission

I’m proudly mixed race, queer, and neurodivergent, a late-diagnosed autistic adult (AFAB) and twice exceptional. My lived experience shapes how I see the world and fuels my mission to build apps that protect people, support emotional wellness, and make the digital space safer for everyone, especially those who don’t always feel seen.

Current Project: AURA

Right now, I’m working on AURA, an AI-powered safety assistant that uses GPT-powered emotional analysis and real-time alerts to help people spot manipulation, scams, and other threats.

Beyond the Code

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me weightlifting, playing chess, or reading up on psychology and human behavior, all part of my mission to blend tech, empathy, and security in every project I create.
